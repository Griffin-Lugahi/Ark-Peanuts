About SweetBite

The story behind every slice

🎂

Our Story

SweetBite was born in a small Nairobi kitchen in 2019, driven by one simple belief — that every celebration deserves a cake as special as the moment itself. What started as weekend baking for family and friends quickly grew into Nairobi's most-loved custom cake shop.

💛

Our Mission

To create beautiful, delicious cakes that make your special moments even sweeter — crafted with premium ingredients, artistic designs, and a whole lot of love baked in.

🌟

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted cake brand — where every order feels personal, every design is unique, and every bite leaves a lasting memory.
