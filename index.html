Our Story

About Ark Peanuts

Ark Peanuts started with a simple idea: snacks should taste like where they came from. We partner directly with smallholder farmers across Kenya, paying fair prices for every harvest and hand-selecting only the best nuts to roast.

Every jar and packet is small-batch roasted, free from preservatives, added oils, and hidden sugar — just the way nature intended. What started as a family kitchen project has grown into a brand trusted by thousands of households across the country, without losing the care that built it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we get asked most. Don't see yours? Ask us directly.

Our roasted nuts stay fresh for up to 3 months unopened when stored in a cool, dry place, and about 3–4 weeks once opened if resealed tightly. Butters and brittles are best enjoyed within 2 months of the roast date printed on the label.

Yes. We deliver nationwide, with same-day and next-day delivery available within Nairobi. Upcountry orders typically arrive within 2–4 business days depending on your location.

All our products contain tree nuts and/or peanuts and are processed in a facility that handles multiple nut types. If you have a nut allergy, please avoid our products or consult your doctor before trying them.

We accept M-Pesa, major debit/credit cards, and bank transfer for bulk or corporate orders. Cash on delivery is available within Nairobi.

We stand behind every jar and packet we send out. Here's how returns work.

2

Share the Details

Send us a photo of the item and describe the issue, damaged, wrong item, or a quality concern.

3

Eligible for Returns

  • Damaged or spoiled items on arrival
  • Incorrect item or quantity delivered
  • Unopened, unused products within 7 days of delivery
  • Quality concerns reported within 48 hours

Not Eligible for Returns

  • Opened food items due to change of mind
  • Items reported after 7 days of delivery
  • Products damaged from improper storage after delivery
  • Bulk/custom orders already processed for dispatch
